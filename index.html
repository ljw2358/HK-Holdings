PHK Holdings

Global Holding Structure for Banking, Robotics, Fusion Energy, and Space Industry

What is PHK Holdings?

PHK Holdings is the top-level holding entity that designs, owns, and governs an integrated industrial ecosystem combining finance, robotics, fusion energy, and space industries.

PHK Holdings does not operate as a single product company. Its role is to structure capital, technology, and public impact into a unified long-term system.

Core Structure

PHK Bank

PHK Bank is the financial backbone of the ecosystem. It provides digital banking, settlement, staking, custody, and future UBI distribution mechanisms.

PiWorldBank (PWB)

PiWorldBank is a public-facing financial platform focused on Pi-based participation, staking, rewards, and global access.

PWB operates under the strategic and structural governance of PHK Bank and PHK Holdings.

PHK Robotics

PHK Robotics develops and industrializes physical AI systems including: industrial robots, logistics robots, medical robots, defense systems, special-purpose robots, and humanoid platforms.

PHK Fusion Energy

PHK Fusion Energy focuses on the commercialization of fusion technology (artificial sun) as a long-term global energy solution.

PHK Space

PHK Space integrates robotics and energy systems into space infrastructure, including launch support, orbital systems, and future off-world industries.

Relationship Between PHK Bank and PiWorldBank

PHK Bank is the primary financial institution. PiWorldBank is a structured public platform operating within the PHK Bank framework.

  • PHK Bank defines financial architecture and compliance
  • PiWorldBank provides participation, staking, and access
  • All value flows are ultimately governed by PHK Holdings

Staking & Participant Protection

Existing stakers are protected within the PHK ecosystem. No value is destroyed or abandoned.

  • Previously staked assets remain recognized
  • Future staking transitions to PHK Bank standards
  • Participants may re-stake under PHK structures if desired

Mission

The mission of PHK Holdings is not short-term profit. It is to design a system where advanced technology serves humanity, stability, and long-term public benefit.
